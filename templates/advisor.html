About Alim Misbullah, S.Si., M.S.

I joined as junior lecturer at Informatics Department, Mathematics and Sciences Faculty, Syiah Kuala University on March 2019. I finished my master degree at Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, National Chiao Tung University in 2015. In October 2015 until January 2019, I worked as Senior Engineer in R&D DaVinci Innovation Lab, ASUSTek Computer Inc., Taipei.
My research areas are focused on Deep Learning for Speech Recognition. I am familiar with some toolkits to build speech recognition system e.g. Kaldi, Tensorflow, and Deep Speech. In addition, I am currently doing research on NLP to build recommendation system using Rasa toolkit.
